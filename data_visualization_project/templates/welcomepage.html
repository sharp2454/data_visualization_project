Overview

The purpose of this project was to analyze the NTSB aviation accident dataset for the United States, its territories, and possessions from January 1, 2019 to Feb 2021 and determine if all aviation accidents result in fatalities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Leaflet to plot and provide more details about the accidents. Details we looked at included: accident number, date of accident, city, state, injury severity, number of injured, type of aircraft, and make of aircraft.
